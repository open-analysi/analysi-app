A **high-severity** alert (SOC175) indicated a possible **CVE-2022-41082 (ProxyNotShell) exploitation attempt** against **Exchange Server 2** on **September 30, 2022**. The attack originated from **IP 91.234.56.6 (South Korea)**, a known malicious actor. Logs showed three blocked requests attempting exploitation, but **no email compromises or suspicious endpoint activity were detected**.

&nbsp;

Threat intelligence confirmed the IP’s association with **SSH brute force and scanning activities**, but **perimeter defenses successfully blocked the attack**, preventing escalation. **No immediate action was required**, but recommended mitigations include **applying security fixes, educating administrators, geo-blocking high-risk regions, and reviewing firewall rules**. The case was documented and closed as **True Positive (Blocked/Prevented)**, with ongoing monitoring advised.
